AREAS OF INTERVENTION ~

Guria works under the following broad areas of intervention:

Education and Alternative Livelihood -

  • Supporting vulnerable children with education
  • increasing access to mainstream schools;
  • enabling victims and potential victims to develop alternative livelihoods.

Healthcare -

  • Ensuring access for vulnerable women/girls and victims

Legal Work -

  • Pursuing legal criminal cases against
  • human traffickers, support and witness protection to victims of trafficking/commercial sexual exploitation/forced labour

Worldwide Awareness Campaign -

  • “Freedom Now” For more information, visit freedomnowcampaign.org
  • Awareness Campaign against Human Trafficking/Unsafe Migration
  • Publications including status reports on human trafficking

Advocacy -

  • Raising awareness amongst decision makers and lobbying for legislative changes which combat human trafficking and exploitation

Rescue -

  • Carrying out rescues of trafficked enslaved persons

Prevention -

  • Building vigilance groups of local women against human trafficking in vulnerable source areas

Sensitization -

  • Programs with police, media, advocates, border police, NGOs, CBOs, judiciary

Cross Border Trafficking -

  • Prevention at Indo-Nepal Border

Networking -

  • Building partnerships with national and international bodies

Pioneered in the year 1993 by Ajeet Singh, Guria, a 36 member NGO in India, works towards eradicating human trafficking and sexual exploitation of women and children.
